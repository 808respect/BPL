Our Strategy

Africa is ripe for venture to late stage capital investments, providing tremendous opportunities for growth, expansion and value adding to the agri-business ecosystem.

Traditionally a mainstay for most African economies, the sector remains critical for the world's fastest-growing populations.

Focus Area

BPL is an investment company focused on the fast-moving consumer goods sector, significant growth area within Africa. We are part of the sector expansion and growth creating quality deal-flow for the ecosystem through innovation and opportunities availed to the entrepreneurs.

Finance

Given this operating and investing model accompanied by deep industry relationships, BPL has access to funding sources from banking, institutional and ultra-high worth individuals.

Expertise

The principals of BPL possess industry operational, technical and investment expertise applied to achieve world class management and growth of emerging producers and manufacturers.
