About

BPL Africa is your investment partner, deploying distinctive capabilities to invest in agri- businesses that are uniquely placed in the international and local value chains.

We have access to entrepreneurs and business with a unique and competitive positioning that helps our partners build and grow sustainable and impactful businesses.

We are long-term partners for growth and impact for brilliant entrepreneurs seeking to bring change and leverage Africa’s economies.

Our purpose is to link focused investments in agribusiness that provide significant impact and value for all stakeholders for a better world.

We aim to establish scale platforms of local and internationally recognized food brand businesses.
